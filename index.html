How to pronounce my name

My first name, Алена (Alena, pronounced "Ah-LEH-nah"), is a common Belarusian variant of the feminine Ancient Greek name Ἑλένη (Helenē). It came to Eastern Europe with the spread of Christianity thanks to St. Helena of Constantinople.

My last name, Буінская (Buinskaya, pronounced "Boo-INN-sky-ya"), is the feminine version of the family name Buinski, originating from western Belarus and northern Poland.
